# The Peachy Rooster

**Red** by The Peachy Rooster is a vintage UK Rice Beaufort Double horse trailer, which has been converted into a unique, one-of-a-kind, mobile pizzeria and bar.​

It features Ontario soft maple countertops, stainless backsplash, a full size commercial bar fridge, and two Gozney Roccbox pizza ovens, allowing us to create fresh, hand crafted, stone baked artisan pizza right on site.

## Hand-crafted pizza & refreshements

We curate your menu to reflect your events theme, from traditional handmade dough to exotic mocktails and refreshments.

Our artisan-style pizzas and signature mocktails use **fresh, local, seasonal ingredients** making your dining and refreshment bar an unforgettable experience.

## Festival & events

Red really draws a crowd and is a **great addition to your special event**. We stock our trailer to suit your event, so there's always something refreshing and tasty for everyone!
